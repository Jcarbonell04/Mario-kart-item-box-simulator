Mario Kart Item Box Simulator

Choose a racer position, then click the picture to simulate opening a Mario Kart Item Box. In theory, when choosing positions 1-6 you will have a 45% collecting a banana, a 35% getting a green shell, 15% for a star, 4% recieving a golden mushroom and only 1% chance collecting bullet bill. When you choose positions 7-12, you will have a 5% collecting a banana, a 5% getting a green shell, 25% for a star, 35% recieving a golden mushroom and a 30% chance collecting bullet bill.
